# Multiplying by 11 
Take the first and last digit and add them together and insert that in the middle of the original digits 

Example:
$$34\cdot11 = 374$$
If the addition adds up into the next digit carry over the value 
$$37 \cdot 11 = 407$$
$$ 38 \cdot 11 = 418 $$
